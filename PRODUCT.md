# PRODUCT.md - Curriculum Management System

## Product Purpose
A centralized platform for managing, tracking, and auditing academic curricula for the Faculty of Science, Naresuan University. It replaces manual document tracking with an automated pipeline.

## Users
- **Faculty Members:** Responsible for curriculum design and uploading TQF documents.
- **Admin:** Educational service officers who audit submissions and manage committee workflows.
- **Executives:** Deans and Vice Deans who review the overall progress of all curricula.
- **Registrar:** University-level officers who access approved documents.

## Strategic Principles
- **Clarity over Complexity:** Curriculum management is complex; the UI should be the opposite.
- **Pipeline Visibility:** Users must always know where a curriculum stands in the approval process.
- **Modern Educational Brand:** Reflect the prestige of Naresuan University with a clean, indigo-driven aesthetic.
- **Mobile Accessibility:** Support quick checks and notifications on the go.

## Register
product

## Tone
Professional, structured, efficient, and reliable.

## Anti-References
- Overly playful or "bubbly" designs.
- Extreme minimalism that hides critical information.
- Legacy "table-heavy" enterprise software.
